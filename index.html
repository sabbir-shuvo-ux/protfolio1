Tushar Roy

I am a Product Design Leader and Coach with a technology background and a passion for inclusive products and teams.

UX Leadership

As a Product Design Manager at VMware, I am responsible for leading the end-to-end experience strategy across VMware Engineering & Multi-cloud Platform services.

In the last 4 years, from being the first designer for VPS, I have built and scaled this 12-member team to span across 3 countries and cater to 20+ initiatives, improving cross-functional collaborations, design process, and increasing UX maturity year after year.

I define Design as “contextual problem solving within constraints”. Great designs are crafted when user needs, business goals, and technology boundaries are brought together unbiased. I believe Design is a team sport, and one of the primary jobs of a designer is facilitation. Great designs are crafted when a team celebrates diverse views, inspires in ambiguity, and creates a psychologically safe environment for creativity to foster on.

Throughout my career, I have learned to use the powers of radical collaboration, DEI, and leading with love (my superpower!) to build and scale diverse teams that thrive around creative challenges.
